Urgent safety

If you are in immediate danger, do not email us.

SoundSense is a supplement, not a replacement for life-safety equipment. In an emergency, call your local emergency number: 911 in the United States. Keep a working smoke detector, a flashing doorbell, or whatever alerting system you already trust in place.
