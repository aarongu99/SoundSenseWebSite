The most important thing

SoundSense is a supplement, not a replacement.

Keep your smoke detectors, hearing dogs, flashing doorbells, bed-shaker alarms, and any other emergency notification system you already rely on. Software can fail. Phones can be dead or out of earshot. SoundSense adds awareness on top of your existing safety setup. It does not replace any piece of it, and we do not want it to.
