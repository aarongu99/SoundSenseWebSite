In memory

Jeff Gordon

Sunshine in human form.

This app is dedicated to my friend Jeff.

Jeff was the kind of person who could bring a smile to anyone's face. The happiest man in the room, even when things were tough. He had a way of making sure nobody felt left out. Everyone was included, always.

SoundSense is built on that idea. Making sure nobody is left out of the sounds that matter.

Grateful I got to know him. Grateful for every day.

Keep living the dream, Jeff.

Aaron Guss
Founder, SoundSense
